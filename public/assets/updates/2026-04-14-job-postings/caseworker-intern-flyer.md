# Caseworker Intern

## About Jawafdehi

**Building Nepal's Institutional Memory Against Corruption**

At _jawafdehi.org_, we're creating an open database of corruption cases for Nepal, making allegations, evidence, and outcomes accessible to citizens. Born out of the GenZ mandate to end the culture of corruption in Nepal, Jawafdehi remains a civic tech accountability platform focused on strengthening public access to information and institutional memory.

Join us in documenting corruption and strengthening accountability through rigorous research and structured data.

## The Role

**Position:** Caseworker Intern  
**Type:** Fully Remote  
**Location:** Anywhere in Nepal  
**Duration:** 3 months  
**Start Date:** As Soon as Possible  
**Compensation:** Both paid and unpaid positions available  
**Commitment:** Part time/flexible

We're looking for a detail-oriented law student to help build a comprehensive corruption case database. You'll investigate allegations from news reports, court documents, and public records, then structure verified information into timelines, evidence records, and case summaries.

## Key Responsibilities

- Study CIAA and other corruption cases and upload them to Jawafdehi after meeting quality standards
